Welcome to Army Tactics

Army Tactics is a passion project that combines elements of games such as Total war, Mini warriors, Mount and Blade, Hearthstone, Team Fight Tactics and many more...

Army Tactics consists of 2 main game modes arena and campagin. Arena will have you choosing your army and battleing it out against the computer in rounds of increasing difficulty, can you make the right choices and outsmart the computer? The rouge-like campagin will challenge you to keep your hero alive as you try to make it as far along the map as you can. There are many encounters, units to recruit and even some special suprises you will find along the way.

Gameplay consists of exciting turn based combat where you will have to think about positioning, unit abilities and unit stats, stratergy is the name of the game. Arena will have you focusing on the units and their abilites while campagin has a bit more depth. You will be rewarded with extra resources upon battle completions which you can use to recruit units and buy items but be smart about your choices as you move along the map enemies will become stronger and harder to beat. If you lose your main character its all over.

Army Tactics is being developed by me and me alone. All feedback and interest are more than appreciated and if you have any ideas or suggestions please feel free to get in contact with me. Art is produced by Katie Bayliss, you can find a link to her work here, I would highly recommend you check out her stuff :).
